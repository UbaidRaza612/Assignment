How can advancements in artificial intelligence impact the job market over the next decade?

  • AI is likely to transform the job market by automating repetitive and routine tasks, which could displace certain jobs, especially in industries like manufacturing, logistics, and customer service. However, it can also create new opportunities in fields like AI development, maintenance, and ethics. Upskilling and reskilling workers will be critical to bridging the gap between job displacement and new opportunities.
